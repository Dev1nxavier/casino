Game Play

  • ANTE

    Select one of four chip values in player console to set the "ante"

  • BETTING

    Click any position on board to place bet. Clicking same position multiple times will increase your bet on that position.

  • SPLIT BETS

    Players can "split" one bet between two number positions.

    • Hold the SHIFT Key while selecting two adjacent numbers
    • Selected numbers will be hilighted in BLUE
    • Press "Split Bet" button in player console to confirm bet
    • Once bet is confirmed, the token icons will appear in both squares signifying successful bet.

  • WHEEL SPIN

    Click the wheel to begin spinning. The landed number will be displayed in the top section of the window

  • WINNING

    Winning bets will be displayed at the top section of the window until the next round of bets. Player cash will automtically update with total winnings

  • MONEY

    If you run out of money, please send credit card information to anonymous@no-where.com ... JK

Payouts

  • Straight (1 number): 35-1 payout
  • Split (2 numbers): 17-1 payout
  • Colours (18 numbers): 1-1 payout
  • Dozens (12 numbers): 2-1 payout
  • Highs/Lows (1-18 or 19-36): 1-1 payout
  • Odds/Evens (18 numbers): 1-1 payout
  • Columns (12 numbers): 2-1 payout
